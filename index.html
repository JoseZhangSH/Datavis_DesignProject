Love at First Hearing

An app that helps beginners pick their loved instrument at first hearing. It allows you to explore and choose your favourite musical instruments through sounds, learn the details of them and even by the instruments online. Also, it can guide you to the nearest instrument stores and enhance the exploration experience of offline stores by playing the sounds of nearby instruments that are around you.

website creation software

Exploration

When you first use the app, you can hear different sounds without knowing which instrument is played. And if you like that sound, click the like button. If you don't like, switch to the next card and listen to another sound.

Instrument
Info

You can check which instrument is played, click the name to check out the details of that instrument and even buy it!

Personal
Information

You can view all the sounds and instruments you liked before in the personal info page and do some other settings here.

Instore
Experience

This app will show a map with several marks indicate where the stores are. When you enter instore mode, it will play the sounds of nearby instruments that are around you.

Instore Scene

When you walk into an instrument store, you can enter instore mode in the app and start to have a fantastic audio experience.

For example, if you walk around a drum set, the app will automatically get your location, play the stereo sound of the drums. As you walk away, the music will gradually disappear until you approach other instruments.

The app also supports multi instruments to play together. If you stand near the French horns and trumpets, you will hear their sounds together based on your location.
